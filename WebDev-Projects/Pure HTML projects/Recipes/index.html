Homemade Red Pasta Sauce

This very simple and classic recipe can be made at home with very few ingredients. Enjoy making this recipe and I can guarantee that everyone will be asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 3 - 4 garlic cloves(finely chopped)
  • (1 3⁄4 cups) tomatoes (2 medium diced)
  • 1/4 cup shallots or onions (finely chopped)
  • 1⁄2 to 1 teaspoons red chili flakes (reduce for kids, adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper freshly crushed (optional + more to garnish)
  • 1 1⁄2 cups (120 grams) fusilli or any other pasta (2 servings - about a cup or macaroni)
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (divided)
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt to cook pasta (+ more to season)

Instructions

  1. Heat a tablespoon oil in a pan and add 1⁄2 cup (70 grams) red bell peppers or white onions and sauté on a medium high heat for 4 mins until blistered.
  2. Add about 250 grams (13⁄4 cups) tomatoes (2 medium diced) to the same pan and saute on a medium high heat until the tomatoes turn mushy. Alternately you may also do this in the oven. Spread tomatoes and bell peppers on a baking tray and add a tbsp. of oil. Bake for 15 mins at 425 F - 220 C, until the tomatoes are softened. Or Air fry for 9 mins at 390F / 200 C.
  3. Add the tomatoes and chilli flakes to a blender
  4. Blend to a smooth consistency
  5. Bring 6 cups of water to a rolling boil and add the pasta. Remember: ALWAYS SALT THE WATER!!
  6. On a medium high heat, cook al dente which is approximately 11 mins
  7. Collect half cup paste water for later use to thicken the sauce. You can drain the pasta with a colander

    8. Making the Red Pasta

  8. Heat a pan with oil and add the garlic. Saute for a minute and the onions.
  9. Saute until transparent. Stir in the chilli flakes and pour the tomato puree.
  10. Saute for 3 to 4 mins and pour the pasta water we had collected earlier
  11. Cook for another 3-4 min until thick. Add herbs, black pepper and salt if needed. Taste test and add sugar if your sauce is too tart.
  12. Stir the pasta into the red sauce
  13. Garnish with parmesian cheese(optional)
  14. Serve and Enjoy!!!!
